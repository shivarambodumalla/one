## Why Resume Format Matters More Than You Think

Even a strong resume can get rejected if the format is wrong.

ATS systems struggle to read:

*   Complex layouts
    
*   Tables
    
*   Columns
    

## The Best Resume Format for ATS

### ✅ Use a Single-Column Layout

This ensures ATS can parse your resume correctly.

### ✅ Use Standard Sections

Always include:

*   Summary
    
*   Experience
    
*   Skills
    
*   Education
    

### ✅ Use Clear Headings

Avoid creative section names.

Use:

*   “Work Experience”
    
*   “Skills”
    

## Best Structure Example

1.  **Header (Name, Contact Info)**
    
2.  **Summary (2–3 lines)**
    
3.  **Skills (Keywords)**
    
4.  **Experience (Impact-focused)**
    
5.  **Education**
    

## Formats That FAIL ATS

Avoid:

*   Two-column resumes
    
*   Infographic resumes
    
*   Heavy design templates
    

These look good but fail parsing.

## Bullet Point Format That Works

Use this formula:

**Action + Impact + Metric**

Example:

> Increased conversion rate by 22% through A/B testing
