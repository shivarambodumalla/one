If you’re applying to jobs and not hearing back, it’s not always about your experience — it’s your resume.

Most resumes never reach a recruiter. They get filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before a human even sees them.

## 1\. Missing Keywords from the Job Description

ATS systems scan for specific keywords. If your resume doesn’t include them, it gets rejected.

### Example:

If the job requires:

*   SQL
    
*   Python
    
*   Data Analysis
    

And your resume doesn’t include these exact terms → rejection.

### Fix:

*   Copy the job description
    
*   Identify repeated keywords
    
*   Add them naturally to your resume

## 2\. Writing Generic Bullet Points

Most resumes sound like this:

> Responsible for managing projects

This tells nothing.

### Fix:

Use impact + metrics:

> Managed 5+ projects, improving delivery speed by 30%

## 3\. No Measurable Results

Recruiters care about outcomes, not responsibilities.

### Weak:

> Worked on improving system performance

### Strong:

> Reduced system latency by 40%, improving user experience

## 4\. Poor Resume Formatting

ATS systems struggle with:

*   Tables
    
*   Columns
    
*   Fancy designs
    

### Fix:

*   Use single-column layout
    
*   Use standard headings
    
*   Keep it simple

## 5\. Not Tailoring Your Resume

Sending the same resume to every job is one of the biggest mistakes.

Each job requires different skills.

### Fix:

*   Customize keywords for each role
    
*   Highlight relevant experience
    

## 6\. Weak Resume Summary

Your summary is the first thing recruiters read.

### Weak:

> Hardworking professional seeking opportunities

### Strong:

> Data Analyst with 4+ years of experience improving reporting efficiency by 25%

## 8\. Overloading with Irrelevant Information

Recruiters don’t care about everything — only what’s relevant.

### Fix:

*   Remove outdated skills
    
*   Focus on role-specific experience
    

## 9\. Using Weak Action Verbs

Avoid:

*   Worked on
    
*   Helped with
    

Use:

*   Led
    
*   Built
    
*   Optimized
    
*   Increased
    

## 10\. Not Optimizing for Keywords + Readability Together

Keyword stuffing doesn’t work.

You need:

*   Natural keyword placement
    
*   Clear readability
    

## Final Checklist Before You Apply

Before sending your resume, make sure:

*   ✅ Keywords match the job
    
*   ✅ Bullet points show impact
    
*   ✅ Metrics are included
    
*   ✅ Formatting is ATS-friendly
