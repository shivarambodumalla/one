## Why Bullet Points Matter More Than Anything

Recruiters don’t read resumes line by line.

They scan.

Your bullet points decide whether you get shortlisted.

## The Biggest Mistake

Most people write responsibilities instead of impact.

### Weak:

> Responsible for managing team

### Strong:

> Led a team of 6 engineers, improving delivery speed by 30%

## The Perfect Bullet Point Formula

Use this structure:

👉 **Action Verb + Task + Result + Metric**

## Examples That Work

### Example 1

❌ Weak:

> Worked on improving website performance

✅ Strong:

> Improved website load speed by 40%, increasing user retention by 18%

### Example 2

❌ Weak:

> Helped with marketing campaigns

✅ Strong:

> Executed 5+ marketing campaigns, increasing lead generation by 25%

## Best Action Verbs

Use:

*   Built
    
*   Led
    
*   Improved
    
*   Increased
    
*   Reduced
    
*   Optimized
    

Avoid:

*   Worked on
    
*   Helped
    
*   Assisted
    

## Add Metrics Wherever Possible

Numbers make your resume credible.

Examples:

*   % improvements
    
*   Revenue growth
    
*   Time saved
