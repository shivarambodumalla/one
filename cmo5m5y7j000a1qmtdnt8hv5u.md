Before a recruiter reads your resume, an ATS scans it.

It checks:

*   Keywords
    
*   Formatting
    
*   Relevance
    
*   Experience alignment
    

If your score is low → automatic rejection.

## Why Most Resumes Fail

*   Missing keywords
    
*   Weak bullet points
    
*   No measurable results
    
*   Bad formatting
    

## How to Beat ATS

### 1\. Use Exact Keywords

Match job description terms exactly.

### 2\. Add Metrics

“Improved performance” ❌  
“Improved performance by 35%” ✅

### 3\. Keep Formatting Simple

Avoid:

*   Tables
    
*   Columns
    
*   Graphics
