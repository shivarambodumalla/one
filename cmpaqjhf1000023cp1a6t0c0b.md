Most resume builders look similar.

Templates, colors, and layouts.

But in 2026, that is not what matters.

Your resume needs to pass ATS systems first and impress recruiters second.

The wrong tool can cost you interviews.

### What Makes a Resume Builder Good

A strong resume builder should help you

- Pass ATS screening  
- Improve content quality  
- Highlight measurable impact  
- Optimize keywords  
- Keep formatting clean  

If a tool only focuses on design, it is incomplete.

### What Most Tools Get Wrong

Many tools focus on how your resume looks.

Very few focus on whether it works.

Common gaps

- No real ATS scoring  
- Generic bullet suggestions  
- Missing keyword optimization  
- Over designed templates

If your goal is interviews, choose a tool that
